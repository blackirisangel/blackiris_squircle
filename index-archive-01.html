Monospaced fonts for programming. This Github page is now the official home of the Proggy fonts. The fonts are all fixed-width and designed for programming. They were optimized while writing C or C++. For this reason, characters like the * are vertically centered as * usually means dereference or multiply, but never 'to the power of' like in Fortran. The {}s are centered horizontally (in case you align braces vertically), the zero looks different from the capital oh, and there is never any confusion between ells, ones, and eyes. Additionally, the arithmetic operators (+ - * <>) are all axis-aligned... unlike the last ones you just saw. Monospaced fonts for programming.
